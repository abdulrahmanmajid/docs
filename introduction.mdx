Kallabot helps you create sophisticated AI voice agents that can engage in natural conversations with your customers. Whether you're handling customer service, sales, or support calls, our platform makes it easy to deploy intelligent voice automation at scale.

## What are AI Voice Agents?

AI voice agents are intelligent virtual assistants that can:
- Conduct natural conversations using advanced language models
- Handle both inbound and outbound calls
- Process and understand customer requests
- Access knowledge bases to provide accurate information
- Execute actions like bookings, updates, and data collection

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Agent">
    Design your voice agent's personality, configure its knowledge base, and set up conversation flows.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    ```javascript
    const agent = new KallabotAgent({
      name: 'Customer Service Assistant',
      language: 'en-US',
      voice: 'emma'
    });
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Launch your agent on our cloud platform or self-host on your infrastructure. Monitor performance and scale as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
